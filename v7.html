our approach

football follows a power law

a few players, clubs, and ideas shape everything. the rest follow.

most play not to lose - chasing stability and control. but in a power law world, the average means nothing.

the outliers change everything

ONE player can change a generation

ONE club can redefine a region

ONE idea can reshape the game

we bet on outliers

we believe the future belongs to those who bet on outliers and build systems that let them compound.

that means backing overlooked talent, daring projects, and new geographies.

we're building a network of football hubs that connect these outliers - places where ideas, players, and opportunities flow freely across borders.

the next leap

the next leap in football won't come from doing things bigger.

it'll come from doing things smarter, earlier, and with conviction.

enable the best people - wherever they are in the world

for clubs that don’t properly enable the best people, no optimisations will matter. we hold this as a core belief for players and staff.

work with truly aligned clubs

we don’t care what average clubs think. we form our own views and accept responsibility for our own taste. we have an incredibly high bar

succession planning

identify, attract, and retain top hearts and minds - passionate characters aligned to a common idea ensures sustainable systems and capacity
